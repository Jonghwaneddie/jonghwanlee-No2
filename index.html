Collaborative project with CYLINDER gallery, focusing on post-human identity and salvaged digital history.

The artist’s first exhibition in London, investigating four-dimensional geometries and their failure in physical space.

An intimate show presenting a series of small-scale sculptures and artifacts related to containment and domestic space.
Waiting Room (Group, 2022).
